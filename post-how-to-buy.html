How to buy a luxury watch online without getting burned

Buying online can be safe if you know what to look for. Here are a few quick checks that help you avoid fakes and bad surprises.

1) Ask about authentication

Look for a clear process (not just “trust us”). If the seller can’t explain how they verify, that’s a red flag.

2) Read the condition notes

“Pre-owned” can mean a lot of things. Check for scratches, bracelet stretch, and whether it was serviced.

3) Watch out for prices that feel too good

If a deal looks unreal, it usually is. Compare with a few other listings to understand the real market range.

4) Check the policies

Insured shipping, a return window, and a warranty (even a short one) help protect you.
